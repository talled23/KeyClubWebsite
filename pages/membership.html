Membership

How do I join this club? Why should I join this club?

How to Join

In order to join, you must be a current student at Bergen County Academies-- no exceptions. In the beginning of the school year, you will recieve a listing of all the morning clubs, and you will see Key Club will be listed there. In order to join, all you have to do is show up at a meeting at the designated time and place in the club list. This will probably be before school every Wednesday in room 231 (but this is subject to change).

Because of the current situation due to COVID-19, we will be hosting virtual meetings on Zoom, using a link which will somehow be distributed to you (probably using an Outlook mailing group which you can join at the beginning of the year)

For reasons to join, please see below

Join an international Community

Key Club is an international organization with clubs on every continent but Antarctica. These are split into 33 districts, with each district split into many different Divisions. Worldwide there are over 270,000 current members.

Plenty of Leadership Opportunities

Need something unique and special to write for college applications? Don't worry! We have leadership positions for the club, the district, and even international! So far we only feel the need for a select amount of club positions (President, Vice, Secretary, Treasurer, Editor, and Webmaster) but these are certainly subject to change and we are looking into more leadership possibilities in the future.

For a complete list of leadership positions, check out this link: here

Get Volunteer Hours

Volunteer hours are not only needed for several BCA opportunities in the future, but are also good for the soul. In Key Club, we have a goal to do internal and external volunteering very often, so that our members can get credit through hours for their help.

Follow in Footsteps of Celebs

Don't trust our opinion? You should join this club to follow a variety of A list celebrities. These include Bill Clinton, Tom Cruise, Brad Pitt, Elvis Presley, and Howard Stern. Follow in the footsteps of an assortment of famous presidents, actors, singers, etc.
