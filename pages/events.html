Events

A list of some of the most recent events we have done so far

Free Rice

Freerice is an ad-supported website and mobile application where users can answer questions on a variety of topics to donate rice to families in need. Go to freerice.com or get the app and use this code (JZ2UJQQ7) to join Division 17's team. So far, our team has donated over 40,000 grains of rice!

(6/2020 - indefinite)

Charity Miles

Charity Miles is an easy way to raise money by simply walking, running, or biking. The app tracks your steps and uses ads to raise money, meaning the only thing you have to do is download it and join our team! Use this link to join our team (through your phone). We've raised over $250 so far!

(6/2020 - indefinite)

Thanking Essential Workers

Our division (div 17) made a video expressing our gratification for essential workers. Members from clubs within our district made signs and short videos that we compiled and shared with our community.

(6/2020)

Holiday Care Packages

Camp Snowflake

Located in Ridgewood, Camp Snowflake is a program which focuses on one-to-one attention and adapted activities for children with intellectual and developmental disabilites. Snowflake meets once per week, on Saturdays from 1:00 pm to 4:00 pm. This is a great way to help out your community and earn volunteer hours, as many of BCA Key Club's members have done.
